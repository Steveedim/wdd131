Cross River State Governor

Meet Prince Bassey Edet Otu

Elected since May 29, 2023

Born on March 28, 1968, in Calabar, Cross River State, Prince Bassey Edet Otu is a distinguished Nigerian politician and the current Governor of Cross River State. He has dedicated his career to public service and development, focusing on improving the lives of the people in his state. With a background in business and public administration, Otu has held various leadership roles, including serving as a member of the Nigerian Senate. His vision for Cross River State includes enhancing infrastructure, promoting tourism, and fostering economic growth to make the state a leading destination in Nigeria.
